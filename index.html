my food philosophy

Food exists to nourish our body.‎‏It heals us and energizes us.

is medicine and eating well is the most basic form of self-respect.

We all value different things when it comes to the food we choose and

there is not a one size fits all. While I believe with all my heart that the food

we eat should be built from roots, Living on Roots is not meant to prescribe

an eating lifestyle to you.‎‏It is my way to provide new, interesting,

ways to get more roots in your life and add more greens, reds, oranges, yellows, and purples on your plate.
